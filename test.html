New Home for CMCS

Everything is in place to start construction on a new home for CMCS. The USDA approved our $6 million loan for facilities and we recently closed on a 6.95-acre tract of land on the west side of Hwy. 17 between Old Plantation Road and Barony Place, near Allston Plantation in Pawleys Island. Designs are being finalized and we hope to start construction soon!

Thanks to a partnership with Coastal Carolina University (CCU), several sixth-grade CMCS scholars will compete in the 2015 Montessori Model United Nations (MMUN) Conference in New York City, March 25 - 28th. This is the second year of what CMCS hopes to make an anchor activity that supports the Montessori philosophy of fostering global citizenship on a sustainable planet.

About Us

COASTAL MONTESSORI CHARTER SCHOOL (CMCS) is a FREE public school choice open to elementary school Georgetown County students, 1st – 6th grade. We are proud be be part of the Georgetown County School District offering a unique combination of Montessori curriculum, charter school governance, and public school access.  

CMCS partners with Georgetown County School District for bus transportation.
